# Sales Analysis and Recommendation

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing data from various sales campaigns to understand the effectiveness of different sales methods. By evaluating revenue performance and customer reach, the project aims to deliver actionable, data-driven recommendations to optimize future sales efforts and maximize ROI.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
* **Understand Effectiveness:** Analyze and compare the performance of three primary sales methods: **Email**, **Call**, and the combined **Email + Call** approach.
* **Evaluate Performance:** Measure overall revenue performance and track customer reach across different segments.
* **Optimize Sales Efforts:** Provide clear, actionable insights and recommendations to help the business allocate resources efficiently.

---

## 📊 Key Business Metrics
To determine the **Best Sales Method**, the analysis focuses on the following key performance indicators (KPIs):
* **Total Revenue:** Total sales volume generated by each method.
* **Conversion Rate:** The percentage of reached customers who successfully made a purchase.
* **Average Revenue per Customer:** The efficiency and financial return of each individual contact method.
* **Customer Reach & Engagement:** The total volume of customers engaged versus those who converted.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Methodology
* **Data Analysis:** Python (Pandas, NumPy) / SQL
* **Data Visualization:** Matplotlib, Seaborn, or Power BI (to visualize revenue distribution and method performance)
* **Key Deliverable:** A comprehensive data narrative detailing the top-performing sales funnel.
